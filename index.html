Carreer

Currently I work as a Technical Consultant at Sopheon, based in Maastricht. I will translate the needs and requirements of the client into the right configuration and will make customizations to our Accolade product if that is desired. In this I am also an advisor on what the best practices are and how to keep the confituration future-proof. All of this requires me one day to be knee-deep in javascript, C# or SQL code, the next day to create complicated Excel documents to create reports and the day after to sit down with the client to design the system.

Software engineering is my true love though. Nothing beats sitting down and coding for hours, hitting Run at the end and seeing that you got it right off the bat (or at least close). I have an interest in automating things as much as possible, leaning against DevOps. Things like Continuous Delivery and automated test running make sure that I can spend more time on actually creating new things, instead of doing deploys manually (with all risks included) or hunting for silly bugs that could have been avoided.
