Career

I currently work as a Technical Consultant at Sopheon, based in Maastricht. I will translate the needs and requirements of the client into the right configuration and will make customizations to our Accolade product if desired. I am also an advisor on what the best practices are and how to keep the confituration upgrade-proof. All of this requires me one day to be knee-deep in javascript, C# or SQL code, the next day to create complicated Excel documents to generate financial reports and the day after to sit down with the client to design the system. It also expects me to know how our software works into detail and be able to identify the root cause of problems from either the configuration or from the core product.

Software engineering is my true love. Nothing beats sitting down and coding, thinking and refactoring for hours, hitting Run at the end and seeing that you got it right off the bat (or at least close). I have an interest in automating things as much as possible, make the computers work for me. Things like Continuous Delivery and automated test running make sure that I can spend more time on actually creating new things, instead of doing deploys manually (with all risks included), hunting for silly bugs that could have been avoided or repetitive administration.
